• Support


    Centennial Park School provides support for our students both academically, and through access to services to assist with mental health.

  • Education



    Centennial Park School caters to highschool students of all levels.

  • Transition


    Centennial Park School is an integrating, non-permanent program that aims to return students to a mainstream setting

  • Community

    Centennial Park School provides access to a wide range of community and support services including counselling, fitness, and study programs.
